My top 5 movies

Drive

Artistic and visually stunning, "Drive" is a neo-noir crime film that features Ryan Gosling as a skilled getaway driver. The film is known for its stylish direction, minimal dialogue, and a pulsating synth-heavy soundtrack.

Inception

A mind-bending thriller directed by Christopher Nolan, "Inception" follows a skilled thief who enters the dreams of others to steal secrets. The film is renowned for its complex narrative structure, stunning visual effects, and thought-provoking themes.

The Dark Knight

Another masterpiece by Christopher Nolan, "The Dark Knight" is a superhero film that transcends the genre. With Heath Ledger's iconic portrayal of the Joker, the film explores themes of chaos and morality, set against a backdrop of Gotham City.

Pulp Fiction

Directed by Quentin Tarantino, "Pulp Fiction" is a cult classic that weaves together multiple interconnected stories. Known for its sharp dialogue, non-linear narrative, and memorable characters, the film has had a lasting impact on pop culture.
